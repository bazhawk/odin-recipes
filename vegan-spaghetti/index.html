Vegan Spaghetti Bologonese

Fills you up goooood

OK - so not actually a bolognese recipe similar to an Italian grandmother but this dish is rich and filling. Great to make on a sunday evening for food prep for the week.

Ingredients

  • 300g of spaghetti
  • 250g sliced mushrooms
  • 250g of red lentils (cooked if possible)
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small white onion
  • 800g of chopped tomatoes
  • Table Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano
  • 100ml of red wine
  • Balsamic vinegar or glaze
  • Olive oil

Instructions

Spaghetti

  1. In a pan, heat water with and add a table spoon of salt. Add Spaghetti and cook for about 12 minutes until spaghetti is cooked

Vegetables

  1. Finely cut onions and garlic
  2. Slice mushrooms evenely
  3. Add oil to the pan and heat on a medium/high temperature. Once oil begins to heat add onions and garlic
  4. Fry for 5 minutes and then add mushrooms
  5. Fry mushrooms with the onions and garlic adding olive oil to avoid mushrooms being dry
  6. Once fried add put all vegetables to the side

Sauce

  1. Heat pan on a high temperature
  2. Add chopped tomatoes
  3. Heat for about 10 minutes stirring frequently until sauce thickens
  4. Add oregano, balsamic vinegar, rice vinegar and wine and stir them in well

And Finally...

Add the vegetables, sauce and spaghetti together
