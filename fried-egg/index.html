Fried Eggs on Toast

Sunny Sundays

Sunday morning and the smell of eggs came hand in hand for me growing up. Over the years I've slightly tailored it to how I like it. I hope you enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized eggs
  • Sunflower oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt
  • Finely cut dill/cilantro/parsley (recommend to only choose one!)
  • A pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat the sunflower oil on a medium to high heat
  2. As soon as the oil begins to pop crack the two eggs over it, side by side
  3. Let the eggs cook for about 4 minutes, checking with a spatula halfway through to see if eggs haven't stuck to the bottom
  4. Add salt, dill and black pepper
